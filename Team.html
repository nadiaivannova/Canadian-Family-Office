John Sanchez

John has been in the financial industry since 1992, helping families and individuals organize their finances and achieve their long term financial goals. He helps his clients get organized and set up a financial road map that will help them reach their financial goals. He became a Certified Financial Planner in 1998 and has focused on helping clients achieve their retirement goals. John takes an educational approach and helps prospective clients by teaching courses on retirement planning and general financial planning. He is also the co author of a book “ Retirement by Design” that outlines all of the key elements of a successful retirement.

In the last 24 years John has worked with many families and individuals helping them with many aspects of financial planning and overall wealth management. He has successfully helped hundreds of individuals reach their golden years with financial independence, in a way that they are able to keep or improve their standard of living well into retirement. With many years of experience John has helped his clients navigate the equity markets by having an investment philosophy that will protect and grow a client’s portfolio over time.

Family & Community:

John is an active participant in his community by coaching youth soccer, volunteering and participating in charitable events throughout the year.

Javier Sanchez

Javier Sanchez has been in the financial industry for over 15 years. He received his CFP (certified financial planner) designation in 1999 from Financial Planners Standards Council and currently manages over 70 million dollars in assets for high net worth investors and families.

Throughout his over 15 years, he has spent much of his time educating the community as a teaching instructor both at the Toronto District School Board and the Toronto Separate School Board. These classes have been very successful at educating the adult student on a wide range of financial matters.

Family & Community:

As a husband and father Javier understands deeply the importance of having a complete holistic financial plan. Involved in the community Javier initiated the first annual CCCO golf tournament which raises money for orphaned children in South America. Through his passion for people Javier plans on continuing his Life based Financial practice and making a difference for years to come.
