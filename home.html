What is a Family Office?

Chief Financial Advisor to Successful Families

A family office acts as a personal CFO or chief advisor to successful families, with a dedicated team of professionals who oversee and manage the complete financial affairs of client families. A family office is not directly involved in the investment/ management of client assets but assists the client in the co- ordination of these services by outside service providers.
